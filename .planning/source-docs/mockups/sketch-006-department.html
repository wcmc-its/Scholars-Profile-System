Medicine

The largest department at Weill Cornell Medicine — over 870 scholars across full-time faculty, affiliated faculty (including NYP-employed clinicians), postdocs and research staff, and doctoral students, organized into eleven clinical and research divisions. Faculty hold appointments at NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital and lead NIH-funded research programs across cardiovascular disease, aging, antimicrobial stewardship, and health equity.

Cardiology

Chief: Eleanor M. Ramirez, MD

The Division of Cardiology at WCM is one of the largest cardiology programs in the Northeast, with concentrations in cardio-oncology, heart failure, electrophysiology, structural heart disease, and cardiovascular imaging. The division leads multiple NIH-funded centers including the WCM Cardiovascular Research Institute.
