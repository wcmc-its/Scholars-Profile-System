# Sketch Manifest — Scholars @ WCM

## Design Direction

Formal, clinical, modern. Information-dense but breathable. Cornell Big Red (`#B31B1B`) accent on near-white surfaces; charcoal text; sans-serif throughout. The product should feel like an institutional asset, not a marketing site. Content first; chrome stays out of the way.

Visual placeholder until WCM institutional UI/branding standards are published (see Project Charter dependency).

## Reference Points

- **Yale School of Medicine** (`medicine.yale.edu/profile/<slug>`) — closest editorial template; modern type, citation counts inline, concept pills per publication, tabbed sub-nav.
- **Harvard Catalyst Profiles / UCSF Profiles** (Profiles RNS family) — feature set inspiration: weighted concept lists, similar people, co-author maps. Phase 2/3 territory; chrome itself is dated.
- **UCSF Profiles landing** — pre-built intent tiles ("Mentor Faculty / Clinical Trials / Global Health Equity") as a stronger on-ramp than a naked search box.

## Anti-references (do not emulate)

- **Stanford Profiles** — metric-thin (no citation counts, no concept treatment, no related people).
- **Hopkins Find-A-Doctor** — patient-rating template; conflates scholarly identity with consumer reviews.
- **Penn Medicine** — fragmented per-department microsites with visible template bugs.
